Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the “QuickCollage” application handles data. Our application is designed as a tool for creating photo collages that performs all operations directly on your device. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and ensuring that no personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted to any external servers.

1. Information Collection and Use

The application does not collect, record, store, or transmit any type of personal data. All interactions within the app—including selecting photos, applying layouts, and saving collages—are processed solely on your device and not shared with us or any third party.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Device identifiers, IP address, or location
  • Analytics or usage activity
  • Your photos or edited collages

2. Permissions

Photo/Media Access: The app requires access to your device's photo gallery solely for the purpose of selecting images to create photo collages. This access is local, and your photos never leave your device.

3. Data Security

Because the application is fully offline and does not transmit data to external servers, the risk of data interception or breach is eliminated. Your photos and collages remain entirely on your device and are subject to your device's built-in security features.

4. Third-Party Services

This application does not use or integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Cloud storage
  • Crash reporting systems

5. Children's Privacy

We comply with COPPA and other international child privacy regulations. Since no data is collected, there is no risk of children's information being captured or stored.

6. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no data to access, modify, delete, or export. You maintain full control over your photos and the application on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in development practices. Updates will be posted in this document and accessible through the app store listing.
