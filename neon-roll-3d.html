Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the “NeonRoll 3D” application handles data. Our application is designed as a tool for 3D dice rolling that performs all operations directly on your device. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and ensuring that no personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted.

1. No Data Collection

The application does not collect, record, store, or transmit any type of data. All interactions within the app—including rolling the dice and viewing roll history—are processed solely on your device and not shared with us or any third party.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Device identifiers, IP address, or location
  • Analytics or usage activity
  • Dice roll results or history data

2. Data Security

Because the application is fully offline and does not transmit data to external servers, the risk of data interception or breach is eliminated. Any visual state or temporary history remains only in your device’s local memory.

3. Third-Party Services

This application does not use or integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Cloud storage
  • Crash reporting systems
  • Social media integrations

4. Permissions

The app does not request access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, storage, or any other personal data. Only minimal permissions required by the operating system to run the Flutter application are used.

5. Children's Privacy

We comply with COPPA and other international child privacy regulations. Since no data is collected, there is no risk of children's information being captured or stored.

6. Open-Source Notice

This application’s source code is part of the "One Week One App" project publicly available on GitHub under the developer’s repository. The privacy guarantees described in this document apply to the official version distributed by Dr. Sapto Labs.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no data to access, modify, delete, or export. You maintain full control over the application on your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in development practices. Updates will be posted in this document and accessible through the app store listing.
