# Privacy Policy for QuickCollage

**Effective Date:** April 29, 2026

DR SAPTO LABS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the QuickCollage mobile application ("the App").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**QuickCollage is designed with a privacy-first approach.** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, photos, or usage data to our servers or any third-party servers.

*   **Photo and Camera Access:** The App requires access to your device's photo gallery solely for the purpose of selecting images to create photo collages. 
*   **Local Processing:** All photo editing, processing, and collage generation are performed locally on your device. Your photos never leave your phone.

## 2. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services that collect your personal data. 

## 3. Data Security

Because your photos and data remain entirely on your device, their security is governed by your device's built-in security features and your personal security practices.

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data, children's privacy is naturally protected.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We implementation update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
